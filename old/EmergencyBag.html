How to prepare an

Emergency Bag

Anemergency go bagis a kit with essential supplies you can quickly grab during emergencies or evacuations, such as food, water, medications, and important documents. Regularly update your go bag, checking expiration dates on food and medications, and rotate items as necessary.

THINGS TO PACK IN AN EMERGENCY GO BAG

  1. First Aid Kit
    • Band-aid, alcohol, personal medicines, paracetamol, ointment, betadine, sterile gauze, medical tape.
  2. Food
    • Bottled water, canned goods, biscuits, eating utensils.
  3. Clothing
    • Clothes (good for 2â€“3 days), slippers, blankets, jacket, underwear.
  4. Tech Equipment
    • Flashlight, powerbank, extra batteries, extension cord, charger.
  5. Toiletries
    • Soap, shampoo, toothbrush, toothpaste, tissue paper.
  6. Identification
    • ID and important documents.
